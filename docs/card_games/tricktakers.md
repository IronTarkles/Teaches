## Overview

A trick taking game where we play characters with different rules and powers who score points different ways

## Stuff

- Must follow
- 1-9 in 4 suits
- Black is trump
- Colorless cards
    - Can be played at any time
    - Rare card is highest card
    - White flag is weakest card
- If mutliple cards of same value are played, first played is considered higher
- Winning conditions
    - two gold crowns
    - 3 black crowns
    - character specific
    - most points at end of 3rd round if none met

## Setup

- Each player gets 30 points
- Crowns are placed on round card
- 5 characters in middle of table
- Everyone is dealt 5 cards
- First player picks their character first, clockwise everyone selects a character
