## Overview

Play all the cards in your hand. If you go out fast you get victory points. If someone gets 4 or more points, the game ends and the player with the most points wins

If playing with three people, remove the 2 cards with 10 on them.

## Setup

Deal out the cards, 8 per player, or 10 per player with 3 players. Make sure to switch up dealing right-side up and upside down

Wait until all cards have been dealt to pick up your hand

Only go off of the numbers on the top of the cards, but you can re-arrange your hand by moving cards to the left and right (just not flipping over)
