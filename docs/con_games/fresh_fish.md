## Overview

We're traders trying to get the best spaces for our market stalls. We're bringing in goods from a few delivery trucks around town, and only the trader with the freshest goods will get the most customers!

## Round structure

Beginning with the starting playing and playing clockwise:

On a turn you have 2 options, either reserve an empty space or draw a tile from the draw stack.

### Reserving an empty space

- Take one of your reservation disks and place it on an empty spot.
    - You must choose an empty spot orthogonally adjacent (no diagonals) to either:
        - A pathway tile (the crossroads)
        - A reservation disc of any player (even your own)
    - A space is empty if there is no disk, no path tile, no delivery truck, no market stall, or no flea market table
    - If you have all of your disks, you MUST take this action

### Draw a tile

The draw stack is made up of two types of tiles. A bunch of flea market tiles, and one stall of each type per player

### Flea market tile

When you draw a flea market tile, it must replace one of your own reservation disks. 

- You will get the disk back to your supply
- Add a flea market stall on top of the tile
- This removes your reservation, but now puts an obstacle to block other player's paths
